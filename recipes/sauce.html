Nigerian Egg Sauce Recipe

Description

This simple egg sauce is an excellent choice when you need a fast, flavourful meal. It's a popular breakfast dish in Nigeria, often served with boiled yams, plantain or bread.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
    • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Bell Peppers
  • Salt, Pepper and seasoning cubes

Steps

  1. Saute onions, tomatoes and bell peppers in a pan with a bit of oil.
  2. Beat the eggs and pour them over the vegetables.
  3. Stir continuously until the eggs are fully cooked and scrambled.
  4. Add salt, pepper and seasoning to taste.
