Dodo

Description

Fried plantains, also known as "Dodo" are a quick and easy side dish or snack. They are sweet, crispy and golden, making them the perfect accompaniment to any Nigerian meal.

Ingredients

  • Ripe Plantains
  • Salt
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Peel and slice the plantains into desired shapes.
  2. Heat oil in a frying pan and add the plantains.
  3. Fry until they turn golden brown on both side.
  4. Sprinkle with a little salt and serve.
